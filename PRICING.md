# LifeDash — Pricing Plan

## Model: Freemium (Free + Pro)

LifeDash is a desktop-first, privacy-focused productivity tool. The free tier is fully functional and not time-limited. Pro unlocks AI-powered automation and data portability features.

---

## Free Tier — $0, Forever

Everything you need to manage projects, capture meetings, brainstorm with AI, and track focus time.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Project Dashboard** | Unlimited projects, boards, columns, cards, labels, drag-and-drop |
| **Meeting Recording & Transcription** | System audio capture, local Whisper transcription, AI meeting briefs |
| **AI Brainstorming** | Conversational AI agent with multi-provider support |
| **Task Structuring Engine** | AI-assisted task breakdown into production-focused steps |
| **Idea Repository** | Capture, tag, and convert ideas into projects |
| **Focus Time Tracking** | Pomodoro sessions, break reminders, session history |
| **Gamification & Streaks** | XP, levels, achievements, daily streaks |
| **BYOK AI (Bring Your Own Key)** | Use your own OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, or Kimi API keys |
| **Privacy & Offline** | Embedded PGlite database, local transcription, zero cloud sync, airgapped capable |

**Key message:** The free tier is not a demo or trial. It's the full core product with no limits on usage, projects, or data.

---

## Pro Tier — You Own It

Unlock AI automation that turns raw data into action — plus full data ownership tools.

**The anti-subscription model:** Unlike SaaS tools that hold your workflow hostage, LifeDash Pro is yours once you pay. If you stop renewing, you keep every Pro feature you had — you just don't get new ones. No lockouts, no downgrades, no "pay or lose your work."

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Card AI Agent** | Per-card AI assistant with tool-calling. Reads card context, suggests edits, researches topics, and takes actions directly on cards. |
| **AI Meeting → Cards** | Automatically converts meeting action items into project cards with the right labels, columns, and descriptions. One click from transcript to task. |
| **Billable Time Export** | Export focus sessions as formatted CSV for client invoicing. Filter by project, date range, and billable flag. |
| **Backup & Restore** | Full database backup to a single file. Restore on any machine. Disaster recovery and migration between devices. |
| **Data Export** | Export all data (projects, cards, meetings, ideas) as JSON or CSV. Your data, your format, no lock-in. |

### Pro Annual — $29/Year

- **$29/year** — Unlocks all Pro features and every update released during your active year.
- **If you don't renew:** You keep every Pro feature at the version you have. The app doesn't lock, downgrade, or nag you. You just stop receiving new features and updates.
- **If you renew later:** You get access to everything released since your last active period. No penalty for gaps.
- Best for users who want ongoing improvements at the lowest price.

### Pro Lifetime — $99 One-Time (No Renewal Ever)

- **$99 once** — Pro forever. Every current and future feature, every update, no expiration.
- Best for users who are done with subscriptions entirely.

### How This Is Different From SaaS

| | LifeDash Pro | Typical SaaS Tool |
|-|:---:|:---:|
| Stop paying | **Keep your Pro features** | Lose access immediately |
| Your data | **On your machine, always yours** | Locked in their cloud |
| Renew after a gap | Pick up where you left off | Start over or pay back-months |
| Price goes up | Your existing license is unaffected | You pay the new price |
| Company shuts down | **App keeps working forever** | Everything is gone |

### Pricing Rationale

- **You're buying software, not renting it.** The $29/year is for updates and new features — not for permission to use what you already paid for.
- **No hostage pricing.** SaaS tools lock your data and workflow behind a paywall. LifeDash can't — your data is local, your app is installed, and Pro features you paid for stay unlocked.
- **No per-seat pricing.** One license, one machine, unlimited use.
- **No cloud costs.** Nothing to host, nothing to scale. Your license key is all you need.

### Team Bundles (Future)

LifeDash is a single-user, local-first tool by design. There are no shared accounts, no cloud sync, and no multi-user features. Each person runs their own instance with their own data.

For teams or organizations, a **Team Bundle** (discounted bulk keys) will be available in the future. Each team member gets their own license key and their own private instance. No architectural complexity, no shared data — just volume pricing.

## Payment & Licensing

- **Platform:** LemonSqueezy (handles checkout, license keys, renewals)
- **License type:** Per-machine activation (one key can activate on one machine at a time)
- **Activation:** User enters license key in Settings > License tab
- **Deactivation:** User can release a machine and re-activate on another
- **Offline grace:** 7 days without internet before license re-validation is required
- **No account required:** No LifeDash account, no cloud login. Just a license key.

- Pro Annual: "$29/year" → same download (trial starts automatically), link to LemonSqueezy checkout

- "Already have a key? Activate in Settings > License"

### Trust Signals
- "Stop paying? Keep your Pro features. We don't hold your tools hostage."
- "Free forever — no bait-and-switch"
- "14-day Pro trial, no credit card"
- "$29/year for updates, or $99 once forever"
- "Offline-first, your data stays on your machine"
- "Open-source core" (if applicable to the public repo)

### What NOT to Say
- Don't say "subscription" without context — always pair it with "you keep your features if you cancel"
- Don't say "limited free version" — the free tier is genuinely full-featured
- Don't hide the free tier or make it feel second-class
- Don't list the price in the hero — let people see the value first
- Don't use "subscribe" or "renew" as CTAs — use "Get Pro" or "Unlock Pro" instead
